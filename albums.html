Kanye West Albums

Kanye West debuted his first album in 2004. It was called The College Dropout. It debuted at number two on the US Billboard 200. This page will showcase all of Kanyes greatest achievements with showing his best albums ranked in order from 1 to 3.




Kanye's best albums are ranked as in order from 1 to 3. With the best album being My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy, followed by The Life of Pablo, and Graduation.

Explanations of the albums

My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy
- The album has clearly had a lasting effect upon the people around it. It pushed hip hop forward not by taking the genre in a new direction, but by taking West's best qualities – production values, sampling and combining collaborators – to new heights.

The Life of Pablo
- Its greatest achievement wasn't just its destruction of archetypal album conventions and willingness to take risks, but also its commitment to a visceral exploration into the mind of a flawed genius just when his year and reputation were about to get worse.

Graduation
- Capturing the feeling of moving on up, and the changes that came with his newfound fame. “Champion” does a great job at juxtaposing his life, in the same verse he talks about shopping so much he should be Italian to a childhood related to Will Smith's Pursuit of Happiness.
